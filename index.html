Featured Projects

Norra Danviksbron, Stockholm

About Our Company

The vision of Pascal Engineering is to be a leading structural engineering firm in the field of advanced steel structures. We strive for our clients to feel their projects are in safe hands and that we exceed their expectations.

We want to inspire and continue getting inspired ourselves. By undertaking technically challenging projects, we allow ourselves to learn new solutions, develop new skills, and build experiences which are transferred to new projects.

By implementing state-of-the-art workflows and modern design tools, we are capable of living up to our promises as well as inspiring the next generation of young engineers who have the same interests and love for engineering as we do!
